# TechnoRouge — Design Plan

## UI / Presentation
- **Title/Login screen:** homebrew-style terminal with custom commands (replacing current class-select grid)
- **Crosshair:** cyberpunk-style reticle (implemented)
- **HUD:** HP, Block, Energy, Deck/Discard counts, enemy display (implemented)

## Core Loop
Explore a seamless open cyberpunk world → find new cards (glowing crystals) → build your deck → fight roaming enemies (proximity-triggered card battles).

## World Structure
- **Seamless open world** — no loading zones, biomes blend as you explore
- **Scale: massive** — takes 10+ minutes on foot to traverse the whole map

## Environments (Biomes)
- Server farms
- Neon-drenched streets
- Data vaults / corporate archives
- Corrupted / glitched zones
- (More to define)

## Card Acquisition
All of the below:
1. **World pickups** — glowing crystals scattered through the environment
2. **Enemy drops** — rewarded after defeating enemies in card combat
3. **Shops / terminals** — spend Cryptobytes to buy cards

## Currency
- **Cryptobytes** — earned from quests and defeating enemies
- Spent at shops/terminals to buy cards

## Quests
All types:
- **Bounties** — hunt down specific enemy types
- **Data retrieval** — find a card/data cache in a specific zone
- **Exploration** — reach certain landmarks / discover locations
- **Story missions** — narrative thread through the zones

## Progression (Roguelike)
- **Between runs:** only special milestone-unlock cards carry over
- Everything else (deck, Cryptobytes, quest progress) resets per run
- Milestones earned by achieving specific goals (defeat a boss, complete quest chains, explore X% of map, etc.)

## Classes (redesign from scratch)
5 archetypes:
1. **Netrunner / Hacker** — bonus effects against status-afflicted enemies; can preview/modify enemy intents
2. **Gunslinger / Merc** — double-hit cards, combo chains, weapon attachments that modify cards
3. **Cyborg / Chrome** — self-damage for power, body mods giving permanent passives per run
4. **Ripper / Doctor** — organic mods that grow over time; healing-to-damage conversions
5. **Corporate Agent** — hire drones/turrets; spend Cryptobytes for on-demand effects

## Enemies
- **Normal roamers** — standard encounters, drop cards + Cryptobytes
- **Elite enemies** — tougher, zone-specific, better rewards
- **Bosses** — unique landmark fights, tied to milestones/story

## Combat System Depth
- **Medium complexity**
  - Multiple statuses (weak, strength, fortify, poison, etc.)
  - Buffs/debuffs with durations
  - Card synergies and strategy
  - Variable enemy intents (attack/defend/buff/debuff/status)
  - X-cost cards, exhaust, ethereal, combo/synergy cards, card upgrades (candidate mechanics)
